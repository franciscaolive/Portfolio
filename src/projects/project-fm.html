FM is my personal brand in development. This project brings together the different iterations of my website as part of that ongoing process. It mostly functions as a record of how I have been improving the way I present myself and my work.

Each version began in Figma, where I could test hierarchy, typography, layout, and rhythm, before translating it into code. The project is as much about design decisions as it is about learning how to build them with precision.

At its core, FM reflects an interest in both self-improvement and self-curation. I see my personal branding not as a continuous process of my intention, taste, and making sure my work is presented with the same level of care that goes into creating it.

The selected screens show that evolution through different approaches to navigation, editorial structure, and tone, while maintaining the same goal: creating a portfolio that is authored, coherent, and aligned with the standard I want my work to meet.
