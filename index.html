About Me

I’m a 21-year-old designer and developer from Porto, Portugal. In 2025, I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Multimedia at ESMAD - Escola Superior de Media Artes e Design. I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Multimedia at FEUP - Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto.

My work centers around design and game development, within the broader landscape of multimedia. With foundations in both art and technology, I am passionate abbout creating innovative ways to merge the two.

Games: Unity, Godot
